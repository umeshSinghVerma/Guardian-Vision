We are the Vigilance Squad

Why should you choose Guardian Vision for your security

Hey there! We guys at Vigilance Squad developed our idea of violence detection system using AI and ML. Our solution is called Guardian Vision and it aims to help prevent and reduce violence in various settings by analyzing video and audio data in real time. We believe that Guardian Vision has a great potential to make a positive impact on society by helping to prevent and reduce violence. We also think that V-Detect can be further improved by adding more features and functionalities, such as integrating with other platforms or devices, expanding the scope of violence detection, enhancing the user interface and experience, etc.

Security Issues?

Violence is a serious problem that affects millions of people around the world every year. It can cause physical, psychological, social and economic harm to individuals, families and communities. However, detecting violence has not been always easy or straightforward. Sometimes, it can be hidden, subtle or ambiguous. Other times, it can be too late to intervene or prevent further damage.

unique solution!

That's when we came up with Guardian Vision, a cam-based software that uses AI and ML models to detect violence in video and audio streams. V-Detect can identify violence, as well as the level of severity and risk. V-Detect can also alert the relevant authorities or stakeholders when violence is detected, such as police, family members, owners.

User friendly :)

To use Guardian Vision, you can sign up or login to our website and upload or stream your video. Our system will then process the data using our trained AI and ML models and provide a report on the violence detection results. Users can also customize their preferences and settings, such as the type of violence they want to detect, the threshold for alerting, the contact details for emergency response, etc.
